ABOUT ME

Hey there! I'm a software developer based in New York City.

I graduated from the University at Buffalo with a degree in finance. After a couple of internships and a full time position, I knew I wanted to make more of an impact on the world around me. I decided to challenge myself and take up web development as a hobby. From building small-scale console appliactions, to coding for hours on end, I knew I found what I was truly passionate about! That's when I decided to take a huge leap of faith and to pivot careers into software engineering.


As someone who is highly focused and attentive to detail, I thrive on building quality systems that surpass end users' expectations. I love problem solving and breaking down complex tasks into smaller, more managable, challenges. This led into my passion for creating easy-to-use, user-friendly websites.


Aside from coding, I love working out at the gym, playing board games with friends, playing spikeball, watching anime and spending time with my family. Feel free to reach out!
